How the recommendation system works

In the modern era, perfume is more than a fragrance—it’s a means of expressing identity and personality. A chosen scent often reflects character, mood, or cultural values, becoming an essential part of daily life. Perfumes consist of three layers—top, middle, and base notes—that gradually unfold after application.

This system leverages Autoencoder models to extract meaningful features from perfume-related data, capturing underlying patterns and reducing dimensionality for more efficient processing. Additionally, it employs BERT (Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers) to deeply understand and interpret user-provided natural language descriptions, allowing the system to grasp context, nuance, and intent. The combination of these advanced machine learning techniques enables the generation of highly accurate and personalized perfume recommendations tailored to individual preferences.
